Privacy Policy for kBQ

Last updated: January 2026

Welcome to kBQ. This app is developed by phantomXworld dev. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our young users.

1. Children's Privacy

kBQ is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect or request any personal information (like name, address, or location) from children. Our app is 100% COPPA compliant.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Any progress or drawing made within the app is stored only on your device and is not sent to our servers.

3. Advertisements

If the app contains ads, we only use Google Play Certified Ad Networks that are safe for families and children.

4. App Permissions

The app may use basic system permissions like Speech Synthesis for voice features, but it does not access your camera, contacts, or microphone.
